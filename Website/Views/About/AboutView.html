About Me

Lee McPherson, Ph.D.

I am a chemistry professor currently teaching at Rowan University and occasionally at local community colleges. In my spare time, I write learn programming in C#, C++, and Javascript/TypeScript/HTML5 while writing apps for the Universal Windows Platform (UWP).

My apps range in topics from chemistry- to encryption- to entertainment-based. I once won Microsoft's 30-to-Launch Windows Phone 7 app writing contest for an app called Molecular Models which aimed to teach basic chemistry principles using 3D visuals that you could rotate and zoom. I have also written tutorials on the (now defunct) Nokia Developer wiki and the MSDN Technet Wiki.
